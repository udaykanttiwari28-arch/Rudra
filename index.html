About Us

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2011

Rudra Construction, a sister concern of JP Infra, was established in 2011 with a vision to deliver world-class RCC civil contracting services. With over 20 years of combined industry experience, we have grown into one of Pune's most trusted construction firms.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery has helped us successfully complete 28+ projects across residential, commercial, educational, and healthcare sectors. With a dedicated workforce of 450+ professionals, we bring precision and expertise to every project.

Commercial Buildings
